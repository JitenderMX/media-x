About Project

Mia by Tanishq aimed to increase foot traffic at their recently inaugurated Agra store while showcasing their latest collection.

By leveraging the power of Influencer Marketing and targeted outreach, Mia by Tanishq captivated the hearts of Agra with the exquisite craftsmanship of its products. This strategic partnership not only amplified foot traffic at the store but also garnered an impressive 570,875 views and reached over 955,446 accounts, firmly establishing Mia by Tanishq's presence in the fashion jewellery industry.
