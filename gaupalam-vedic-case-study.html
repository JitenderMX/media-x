About Project

A tailored suite of services to address two primary challenges: educating consumers about their offerings and generating leads from specific delivery cities. Our strategy encompassed a blend of digital and offline tactics, including social media management on LinkedIn and Instagram, dynamic collateral development, targeted lead generation campaigns, offline kiosk designs, and influencer marketing. Additionally, we provided comprehensive support with photoshoots and packaging design, ensuring that Gaupalam Vedic's visual identity aligned seamlessly with its brand positioning.

We successfully bridged the gap between consumer education and lead acquisition. The result was heightened brand visibility, increased consumer engagement, and tangible lead generation outcomes, positioning Gaupalam Vedic for continued success in its target markets.
