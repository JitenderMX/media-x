Job Description

As a Brand Strategist at MediaX, your primary role would be to think & draft digital strategies keeping in mind the current market scenario, existing strategy, the scope of work, upselling possibilities, and execution on the basis of briefs. 

May include but are not limited to

  • Effectively manage accounts assigned, jointly strategies for campaigns and take care of end-to-end client co-ordination along with client servicing team.

  • Consult with clients on a range of strategic brand initiatives, including brand positioning, brand architecture, brand analytics, and digital brand strategy.

  • Create and direct innovative and thoughtful strategic solutions on behalf of your portfolio of clients & new business pitches.

  • Develop and articulate strategies that inspire innovative work, including brand strategies, comprehensive multichannel plans, and ad hoc assignments.

  • Collaborate with creative teams to shape creative expression and execution.

  • Balance multiple simultaneous projects and assignments.

  • Monitor Digital & New Media industry trends and competitor offerings to identify potential business avenues and improve current offerings.

  • Actively sample check POA’s & strategy to ensure content is going up as per the schedule and quality levels are maintained.

  • Spot revenue contribution by reporting new opportunities with a brand and/or cross-selling allied services via day-to-day interaction.
